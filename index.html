Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a website take to build?

Most websites take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. We always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do you work with businesses outside Johannesburg?

Yes — we work with clients across South Africa and internationally. All our work is done remotely.

What do you need from me to get started?

A brief about your business, your goals, examples of sites you like, and your logo/brand assets. We guide you through everything.

Can I update my website myself after it's built?

Yes. We build on systems you can manage yourself, and we provide training and documentation on delivery.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We require 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For larger projects we can discuss milestone-based payments.

What makes NM Webworks different from other agencies?

We're a lean, senior-level team — you deal directly with the people building your product, not account managers. We focus on results, not just deliverables.
